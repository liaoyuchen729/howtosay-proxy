隐私政策 · Privacy Policy

中文

为了把你输入的内容翻译成英文并做词汇与语法解析,本 App 会将你输入的文本通过我们的服务器转发给 OpenAI 进行处理。我们不收集你的姓名、账号、邮箱或设备标识,不进行任何广告追踪。

你的单词本、翻译历史和设置仅保存在你的设备本地,不会上传到我们的服务器。

当你点击「报告问题」主动提交反馈时,你选择上报的原文与译文会被用于改进翻译质量,该提交不含任何身份信息。

本 App 的日语词典数据来自 JMdict/EDICT(EDRDG),中文词典数据来自 CC-CEDICT,均依据 Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike(CC BY-SA)许可使用。

English

To translate your input into English and analyze its vocabulary and grammar, this app sends the text you type to our server, which forwards it to OpenAI for processing. We do not collect your name, account, email, or device identifiers, and we do not perform any advertising tracking.

Your vocabulary book, translation history, and settings are stored only on your device and are never uploaded to our servers.

When you actively submit feedback via "Report a problem", the source and translated text you choose to report are used to improve translation quality. Such submissions contain no identifying information.

Japanese dictionary data is from JMdict/EDICT (EDRDG) and Chinese dictionary data from CC-CEDICT, both used under the Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike (CC BY-SA) license.

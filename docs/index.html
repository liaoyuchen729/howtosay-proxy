一、隐私政策(中文)

1. 概述

「想说就译」(以下简称「本应用」)是一款帮助你把母语表达转换为地道英文并进行词汇、语法学习的工具。我们高度重视你的隐私,并在设计上尽量减少数据的收集与传输。本政策说明本应用如何处理与你相关的信息。

2. 我们处理的信息

为实现翻译与学习功能,当你发起一次翻译或提交反馈时,本应用会将你输入的文本内容通过我们的服务器转发给第三方大语言模型服务商(OpenAI)进行处理,并将结果返回给你。该文本属于「用户内容」,仅用于:

  1. 实时生成翻译、释义、例句与语法解析;
  2. 以匿名形式缓存与统计,用于改进翻译与语法识别质量。

3. 我们不收集的信息

  1. 我们不收集你的姓名、账号、邮箱、电话或任何可直接识别你身份的信息;
  2. 我们不收集设备标识符,不进行任何广告或跨应用追踪;
  3. 上述处理的文本内容不与你的身份相关联。

4. 本地存储的数据

你的单词本、翻译历史和应用设置仅保存在你的设备本地,不会上传到我们的服务器。卸载应用或在设置中清除数据即可删除它们。

5. 第三方处理

本应用使用 OpenAI 的接口完成文本生成。你输入的文本会被发送至 OpenAI 进行处理,其处理受 OpenAI 自身的政策约束。我们仅传输完成翻译所必需的文本,不附带任何身份信息。

6. 反馈数据

当你主动点击「报告问题」提交反馈时,你选择上报的原文与译文会用于改进翻译质量。该提交不含任何身份信息,且完全由你自愿发起。

7. 数据保留

用于改进质量的匿名缓存按需保留;由于其不含身份信息,无法、也不会被用于识别个人。本地数据由你自行掌控。

8. 儿童隐私

本应用面向一般用户,不针对 13 岁以下儿童设计,也不会有意收集儿童的个人信息。

9. 你的权利

由于我们不收集可识别你身份的信息,本应用不存在与你账号绑定的个人数据。你可随时通过清除本地数据或卸载应用来移除设备上的全部信息。如有疑问,可通过下方邮箱联系我们。

10. 政策变更

我们可能会更新本政策。重大变更将通过更新本页面顶部的「生效日期」予以公示。

11. 联系我们

I. Privacy Policy (English)

1. Overview

"How to Say" ("the App") is a tool that helps you turn expressions in your native language into natural English and study vocabulary and grammar. We take your privacy seriously and minimize data collection and transmission by design. This policy explains how the App handles information related to you.

2. Information We Process

To provide translation and learning features, when you request a translation or submit feedback, the App sends the text you enter through our server to a third-party large language model provider (OpenAI) for processing, and returns the result to you. This text is "user content" and is used only to:

  1. generate translations, definitions, example sentences, and grammar analysis in real time;
  2. cache and aggregate anonymously to improve translation and grammar-recognition quality.

3. Information We Do Not Collect

  1. We do not collect your name, account, email, phone number, or any information that directly identifies you;
  2. We do not collect device identifiers and do not perform any advertising or cross-app tracking;
  3. The text processed above is not linked to your identity.

4. Data Stored Locally

Your vocabulary book, translation history, and app settings are stored only on your device and are never uploaded to our servers. Uninstalling the App or clearing data in Settings removes them.

5. Third-Party Processing

The App uses OpenAI's API for text generation. The text you enter is sent to OpenAI for processing, subject to OpenAI's own policies. We transmit only the text necessary to perform the translation, with no identifying information attached.

6. Feedback Data

When you actively submit feedback via "Report a problem," the source and translated text you choose to report are used to improve translation quality. Such submissions contain no identifying information and are entirely voluntary.

7. Data Retention

Anonymous caches used for quality improvement are retained as needed; because they contain no identifying information, they cannot and will not be used to identify any individual. Local data remains under your control.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for a general audience, is not designed for children under 13, and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Your Rights

Because we do not collect information that identifies you, there is no account-bound personal data held by the App. You may remove all information on your device at any time by clearing local data or uninstalling the App. For questions, contact us at the email below.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Material changes will be indicated by updating the "Effective date" at the top of this page.

11. Contact Us

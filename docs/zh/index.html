How to Say (Chinese) helps learners say what they mean in natural Chinese — four tones of politeness, word-by-word alignment, tap-to-see pinyin, a word book, and Chinese grammar practice. Interface available in English, Japanese, Korean, Vietnamese, Thai, Indonesian, and Spanish.

帮助学习者把想说的话变成地道中文——四种语气、逐词对齐、点词看拼音、单词本、中文语法练习。界面支持英语、日语、韩语、越南语、泰语、印尼语、西班牙语。
